# AEI / Elsevier submission checklist

## Completed manuscript and evidence checks

- Canonical source is breeze/paper/main_cas.tex; compiled artifact is
  breeze/paper/main_cas.pdf.
- The manuscript uses Elsevier CAS cas-sc and cas-model2-names.
- All numerical tables are regenerated by
  breeze/scripts/build_paper_tables.py from frozen CSV files with row-grid,
  pairing, direction, and pass-pattern assertions.
- The one-sided verifier predicate is split into explicit interval, upper, and
  lower gate sets. Source-code semantics and manuscript equations pass the
  dedicated gate-semantics tests.
- Synthetic-to-real NRMSE, maximum cross-correlation, and byte-identity are
  audited separately from within-pool diversity. Berkeley is explicitly
  labelled as train-only exemplar-background simulation.
- PU/CWRU kurtosis-W1 and fault-frequency-alignment values are fully reported,
  including comparator-favouring cells.
- The PU boxplot real reference uses all 1200/1202/1444 class-matched
  outer-training windows, matching the W1 reference population.
- The global BH sensitivity analysis covers 102 core hypotheses and preserves
  all registered Holm pass/fail decisions.
- The manuscript defines the statistical unit as paired few-shot subset plus
  CNN initialization around one fixed shared pool per method; independent-pool
  inference is not claimed.
- Random open-loop is labelled an open-loop control. Random plus verifier is
  reported as a zero-admission capacity failure.
- The closed-loop admission figure is backed by a 450-record SHA-256 manifest;
  first-pass aggregation exactly reproduces 286 frozen admitted slots and is
  described as complete accounting for one pool-generation run.
- Abstract, Introduction, Discussion, Conclusion, cover letter, highlights,
  and supplementary outline use the same training-free, auditable,
  LLM-mediated recipe-selection claim.
- Berkeley reports both the complete pass pattern and the 0.2--0.5 percentage
  point lower-shot effect over rule.
- PU LOCO v1--v6, UMich condition/process-metadata confounding, and the MU-TCM
  inner-validation stop remain visible.
- The final PDF has 21 pages and 46 resolved references. No undefined citation
  or cross-reference remains.
- Ten manuscript figures are vector PDFs; the full 21-page Poppler render has
  been checked for blank pages, clipping, overlaps, and blank figures.

## Sole submission blocker: author-supplied metadata

- Replace Author One/Author Two, Department/City/Country, short author line,
  and suppressed ORCID records with the approved author order, affiliations,
  ORCIDs, corresponding author, and email.
- Confirm author-supplied funding, contribution, approval, and
  journal-required declaration metadata.

## Non-blocking release action

- Primary CWRU/Berkeley pools, recipes, and detailed records are currently
  present only under ignored breeze/runs/ paths. Deposit them as a
  checksum-indexed GitHub release or data-repository supplement and add its
  persistent identifier before publication. The manuscript states the current
  limitation accurately.

## Claim boundaries that must remain explicit

- No state-of-the-art or superiority-to-trained-generator claim; formal
  TimeGAN/DDPM comparison is future work and smoke values are absent.
- No zero-real-data diagnosis or formal physical-correctness claim.
- No independent-pool, universal-backbone, cross-specimen, or cross-machine
  inference.
- No strict four-fold CWRU LOLO, successful PU condition-transfer, or generic
  milling claim.
- Original PU provider version, top-p, and provider-side sampling seed remain
  unavailable; replay starts from archived recipes and renderer seeds.
